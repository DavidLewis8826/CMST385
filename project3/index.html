Shawn Carter aka Jay-Z born 12/4/1969 estimated a current net worth of 2.4 billion USD. He grew up can came out of the Brooklyn projects. Rap was what he used to escape from the world that surrounded him. Even one of his first songs was able to be featured on MTV. In 2003 he "retired from Rap" Luckily he came back in 2006. In his comeback, he has made so many timeless hits and music that will be around forever. Jay-Z of course is married to Beyoncé who is popular and famous on her own accord. In 2021 Jay-Z was inducted into the Rock & Roll Hall of Fame. Jay-Z's music will have a lasting impact on our world.
