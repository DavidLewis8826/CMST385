Lastest News!

Recently, at the 2024 Grammys Jay-Z made quite the statement when he went up to accept his Dr. Dre Global Impact Award. He started off thanking Dr. Dre for opening the door for the black community. Mentioning all the records they wrote and the covers they helped the black community get on. After that, he thanked the Black music collective for the work they have done with the community.

From there he started talking about the first black people to win a Grammy and how they boycotted it. This is when things started to get to him making a statement. He goes on to say how he boycotted the first Grammy he was put up for because another artist had had some great success with making hit songs and was not nominated for anything.

The crowd the clapping during this and he remarked you don't have toclap for everything you know. Then, in the biggest statements he brings up how his wife Beyoncé has won the most Grammys but never won album of the year. He pointed out how this means by their metric they are wrong. in determining who should win these awards. He wrapped it up by saying you have to keep working and show up till you get what you deserve.
